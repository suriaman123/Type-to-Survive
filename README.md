# Type-to-Survive

**Type to Survive** is a fast-paced browser-based typing survival game where your keyboard is your weapon. Enemies spawn from all directions with words above their heads— type correctly to eliminate them before they reach you.

---
## Game Concept
You start in the center of the screen with **10 HP** and your goal is to survive as long as possible.
Enemies spawn continuously from all directions at random intervals. Each enemy has a word above its head. To defeat it, you must type the word correctly.
As the game progresses:
- Words become harder
- Enemies become faster
- Enemies gain more health
- Some enemies require multiple words to destroy

The game runs indefinitely until the player dies or quits.
---

## Gameplay 
1. Click the **Start Button** in the center
2. Type enemy words to shoot them
3. Survive increasing difficulty
4. Earn upgrades over time
5. Defeat bosses and choose rewards

---

## Player System
- Starts with **10 HP**
- Takes damage when enemies reach the player
- Game ends when HP reaches 0

---

## 📈 Progression System
Every minute:
- +1 Skill Point awarded

Skill points can be used to upgrade:
- Health
- Damage
- Enemy speed reduction

Difficulty increases over time:
- Faster enemies
- Stronger enemies
- More complex words
- Higher spawn rate

---

##  Boss System
Every **2 minutes**, a boss appears:
- Requires typing a **full sentence** to defeat
- Higher HP and increased difficulty

After defeating a boss, the player selects a reward card.

---

## Card Reward System
After each boss, choose **1 of 3 cards**:
### Example Cards:
- Extra Fire – faster and multi-hit shots
- New Gun – increased damage
- Auto Rocket – fires every 30 seconds at the nearest enemy
- Time Slow Field – slows enemies periodically
- Regeneration – slowly restores HP
- Critical Typing – chance for double damage
- Magnet Shot – auto-targeting bullets
- Freeze Burst – chance to freeze enemies

Only one card can be selected.

---

## Difficulty Scaling
Every minute:
- Word difficulty increases
- Enemy HP increases
- Enemy speed increases
- Spawn rate increases

---

##  Player Avatar
- Player is displayed as a circle in the center
- Users can upload any image as their avatar

---

## Game Over
The game ends when:
- HP reaches 0
- Player quits the game

Score is based on:
- Time survived
- Enemies killed
- Bosses defeated
